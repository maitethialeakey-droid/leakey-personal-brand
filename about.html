About Leakey Trading Insights

Welcome to Leakey Trading Insights

Leakey Trading Insights is an educational blog focused on Smart Money Concepts (SMC), ICT trading strategies, and market structure analysis. Our mission is to simplify institutional trading concepts and provide structured learning resources for traders worldwide.

Publisher Identity

This website is owned and managed by Leakey Maitethia, a trading educator and systems developer with expertise in:

  • Smart Money Concepts (SMC) and ICT methodology
  • Market structure analysis and price action
  • TradingView indicator development (Pine Script)
  • Python trading automation and algorithmic systems
  • Risk management and position sizing

Our Mission

To educate traders on market structure, liquidity, and algorithmic trading principles through high-quality, accessible educational content. We believe that understanding how institutional traders operate is key to becoming a consistently profitable trader.

Content Focus

Our blog covers the following topics:

  • Smart Money Concepts (SMC): Understanding institutional trader behavior and market manipulation
  • ICT Trading Strategies: Inner Circle Trading methodology and advanced market analysis
  • Market Structure Analysis: Identifying swing highs/lows, breaks of structure, and key levels
  • Fair Value Gaps (FVG): Trading unfilled price gaps with high probability
  • Risk Management: Position sizing, stop losses, and money management
  • Technical Development: Building custom indicators and trading systems

Educational Purpose

Important: All content on this website is for educational purposes only. We do not provide financial advice, investment recommendations, or trading signals. Trading financial markets involves substantial risk, and you should only trade with money you can afford to lose.

Why We Started This Blog

Most trading education is either too basic or too expensive. We created Leakey Trading Insights to bridge that gap—providing professional-grade trading education that's accessible to everyone, regardless of their experience level or budget.

Our goal is to help traders understand the principles that professional traders use, so they can make better trading decisions and build sustainable, profitable trading systems.

What You'll Learn Here

  • How to identify key support and resistance levels
  • How to recognize market structure shifts and trend changes
  • How to use Fair Value Gaps in your trading strategy
  • How to build custom TradingView indicators
  • How to automate your trading with Python
  • How to manage risk and protect your capital
